Computer Vision

Computer Vision:

  • Computer Vision is a field of study that enables computers to interpret and understand the visual world.
  • It focuses on developing algorithms and techniques for machines to gain a high-level understanding from digital images or videos.
  • Computer Vision is a key technology in various real-world applications such as autonomous vehicles, facial recognition, medical image analysis, and more.
  • Below are some key aspects of Computer Vision:
  1. Image Processing: Involves techniques to enhance, analyze, and manipulate digital images.
  2. Object Detection: Identifies and localizes objects within an image or a video.
  3. Image Classification: Categorizes images into different classes based on their visual content.
  4. Feature Extraction: Extracts meaningful information or features from images for further analysis.

Computer Vision plays a vital role in many cutting-edge technologies and research areas. Understanding the fundamentals of Computer Vision can open up exciting career opportunities in fields such as artificial intelligence, robotics, and image processing.
